About


An integrated deep tech platform that leverages data from a network of remote sensors and satellites to provide real-time monitoring and predictive analysis of climate change impacts. This system will enable informed decision-making for disaster preparedness, resource allocation, and mitigation efforts in response to changing environmental conditions.

How to Use ?


Welcome to the ClimateChange Monitoring platform. Here's how you can use our platform effectively:


  • Explore Data: Access real-time data from remote sensors and satellite imagery. Use the interactive map to explore different data layers and geographical regions.

  • Analyze Trends: Dive into historical data and use our analytical tools to identify trends, anomalies, and climate change impacts.

  • Predictions: Our advanced machine learning models provide predictions on climate change impacts. Use these insights for decision-making.

  • GIS Integration: Leverage our Geographic Information System (GIS) for spatial analysis and visualization. Understand how environmental changes impact specific locations.
